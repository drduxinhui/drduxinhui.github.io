Primary cell culure protocol by peter 2023.10.11

Materials

4 degree water and container.

Preparation in the OR

  • Get fresh samples, wash with strile 0.9% NS and mince with surgical blade to get 1mm3 fragments.

  • Use 20 or 50 ml syringe to transfer the tissue fragment to 15ml tube.

  • Put the sealed tube inside transfer container with 4 degree water.

  • Transfer the container to lab for next step. (ASAP.)

Preparation in the lab

  • Centrigue at 1500r x 10min, collect the pellet and resuspend with DMEM full medium (with 10% FBS and antibiotics)

  • Spread the tissue fragments in the flask and put it inside the incubator.

Routine culturing

  • Check daily for tumor cell growth and fibroblast outgrowth.

  • Change medium for every four days.

  • Passage based on the confluence of the cells.

Use differential trypsinization and multiple passage method to get rid of the fibroblasts.

  • Store the cells every 10 passages and keep the cell lysate every 11 passages for validation studies.

  • Keep records of the cell morphology every two passages.

  • Validation of the cell line after 6 months of culturing.

Replacement of old medium by new complete media was also done based on the color change of media.
If microbial contamination was detected in any dish or flask, the entire content was immediately discarded.
Chage the medium while keeping the explant tissue for about 1 week untile the primary outgrowing cells reached around 70-80% confluency in the culture flasks.
